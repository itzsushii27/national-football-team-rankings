Enter Results

One line per team that played ≥1 match this cycle. Format:
Name.PreviousScore.(OppRank.Importance.Result)x5
Result is W, D, or L. Give as many parenthetical groups as matches played this month (1–5). Ranks are raw FIFA ranks (1–210) — normalization happens automatically.
Example: Morocco.13.7.(8.3.D)(30.4.W)(3.5.L)
This overwrites scores only for teams listed above. Everyone else stays unchanged.
